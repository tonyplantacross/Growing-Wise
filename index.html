Who we are

GrowingWise is a Leeds based company offering high quality education through gardening. Our aim is to educate and improve children’s lives by connecting them to their environment and helping children to discover their role in maintaining and improving it, for their benefit and for others.

Covid-19 Information

GrowingWise offers support for schools at a time when some children may find re-engaging in the learning environment challenging.

In line with your needs, a learning program featuring a range of activities is created to provide support for a ‘gap’ curriculum.

  • Learning outdoors in a meaningful context. This may help children that have not had many opportunities to go outside recently.
  • A catch up on core science knowledge, such as creating experiments and recording data.
  • An opportunity for application of skills and consolidation of knowledge, such as English -describing plants and writing out labels. Maths - measuring out a planting area or the number of bugs found.
  • Developing co-operation and working together.
  • A fun way of learning again in the school environment.

Each school is different and a plan is created based on your needs and setting.

How it works

For safety and best practice the activities are done in small groups. The class is split in half. One half stays in school, whilst the other half completes the activity. With a Teacher or TA present and required, Tony from Growing wise will lead the activity. All activities can be done outdoors but if the weather is too bad there are alternatives for the classroom and the whole class to enjoy. All activities are risk assessed and are updated with covid -19 guidelines. Full clean DBS.
